%%SECTION: HERO / OPENING%%
### Support the Work

Christ the Reconciler runs on prayer, labor, and the generosity of people who believe that the unity of the Body of Christ is worth working for.

Your gift makes it possible to keep the door open — for retreatants, for the community, for the teachings, and for the work of reconciliation that flows outward from this place.

---

%%SECTION: WHAT YOUR GIFT SUPPORTS%%
### What Your Gift Supports

**The common life** — daily prayer, shared meals, the rhythms of a household devoted to John 17.

**Hospitality** — keeping the ministry center open for guests, retreatants, and anyone who wants to come and see.

**Teachings and resources** — producing and maintaining the teaching library, the podcast archive, the newsletter, and the website — all offered freely to the wider Church.

**The land** — stewarding the sixteen acres in Elgin toward its vision of becoming a visible and visitable example of lived reconciliation.

**Phase 3** — the next step of physical development: residential houses, a prayer chapel, and expanded shared spaces, planned for 2027.
