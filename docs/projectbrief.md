- **What is this site for? Who is it for?**
  - This is a site of an ecumenical group, "Christ the Reconciler". They describe themselves as "A Missional Community of Reconciliation involving Protestants, Catholic, Orthodox, and Messianics Jews." The site is intended to reach current community members, potential new members, folks doing other similar work, including other intentional communities.
